Privacy Policy

Effective date: 28 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Easy Smoko ("we", "us") collects, uses, and stores your information when you use the Easy Smoko app ("App"). Easy Smoko is operated under Australian privacy law (Privacy Act 1988).

What We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name and email address (account registration)
  • Business details (ABN, phone, address, bank details for invoices)
  • Client names, addresses, and contact details you enter
  • Job, quote, and invoice data you create
  • Voice and audio data when using the Ol'mate voice assistant (processed by Anthropic and ElevenLabs)
  • Photos you attach to jobs

Automatically collected:

  • App crash reports and error logs (via Sentry)
  • Basic usage data (via Expo/EAS)
We do not collect precise device location.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Provide and operate the App (store your jobs, invoices, quotes, and clients)
  • Process voice commands through Ol'mate
  • Generate PDF invoices and quotes
  • Sync with accounting integrations you connect (Xero, MYOB) — only when you explicitly connect them
  • Send transactional notifications you request (job reminders, invoice due dates)
  • Diagnose crashes and improve the App
We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data.

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase (servers in the United States). By using the App you consent to this transfer. We use industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Voice audio sent to Ol'mate is processed by Anthropic (Claude) and ElevenLabs and is subject to their data retention policies. We do not store raw audio recordings ourselves.

We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Accounting Integrations

If you connect Xero or MYOB, invoice data is transmitted to those services on your behalf. You control this connection and can disconnect at any time from Settings. We only send data you explicitly create in the App.

Your Rights

Access

All your data is visible within the App at any time.

Export

Contact us and we'll provide a copy of your data.

Delete

Delete your account via Settings → Delete Account or email us. Processed within 30 days.

Correct

Edit your data directly in the App at any time.

Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Backups may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion.

Voice data processed by Anthropic and ElevenLabs is subject to their own retention policies.

Children

The App is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at contact@easysmoko.com.au and we will delete it.

Security

We use TLS encryption for all data in transit and rely on Supabase's SOC 2-compliant infrastructure for storage. Access to your data is protected by row-level security — only your account can access your business data.

Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you via the App for material changes. Continued use after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
